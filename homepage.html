University of Washington

UW Career & Internship Center

Great databse of resources for University of Washington students. There is information on career and internship fairs, advice on how to set-up accounts at important career-advancing services such as LinkedIn or Handshake, as well as identity resources for marginilized communities.



Daniel Herb

Internship Success Manager

"As the Internship Success Manager, I am excited to help students explore, find, and acquire high-quality internship experiences. I mainly work with UW staff, campus advisers, and employers to develop programs and processes that ensure students can access and thrive in these meaningful outside-the-classroom opportunities."



Magdalena Balazinska

Director and Professor, Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science & Engineering

Magdalena Balazinska is Professor and Director of the Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science & Engineering at the University of Washington. Magdalena's research interests are in the field of database management systems. As an advisor she will be able to connect you with potential internship and career opportunities as well as offer invaluable advice on what employers look for in a resume.
