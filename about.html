About Us

Due to the recent global pandemic, we have seen a massive labor shortage and a push to work-from-home. People everywhere are struggling to make the jump to new careers or the transition to remote work. They want to enter the workforce prepared but are lost as to how to begin. And that's where we come in. Jumpstart is a one stop shop for career advice and information in our new world. We have resources are available for those looking to switch careers or make the move to full-time remote employment.

Servicing Our Community

Our focus on making these consolidated resources accessible to everyone, so we operate as a non-profit. To offset the eventual costs, we would charge companies a fee to have more features on our site. Due to the staffing shortage, companies would find it advantageous to be on our website and gain exposure to potential employees.
We will also host workshops in community centers to make our services available to those without reliable internet access.

Our Pages

We designed this page with the intention of helping people easily find the resources they need to discover and enter new career fields. We also wanted to help make the move to working from home, whether full or part time, as smooth as possible. We cover everything from home office setup to online programming courses to how to find the perfect entry-level job.

Resume Reviewer: Our resume reviewer is designed to give our users the most customized and detailed feedback possible. With experts and advisors from every industry, users will come away with a potential career path and a clear idea of how to move forward with their career journey, whether that be a community college class or an internship.

Job Search: Our job search feature gives users with more work experience the ability to search for specific jobs that match their specific needs. Filter by industry, location, and remote preference to receive available job listings. All job listings on our website are required to clearly specify if they have remote options or flexible work schedules.

Email List: Make sure to sign up for our email list and customize your preferences to get notified when new opportunities become available. Whether you want to hear about jobs, free programs, or everything in between, we will be sure to notify you as soon as they get posted!
